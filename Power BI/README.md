# Power BI Dashboards

Exported Power BI reports (PDF) showcasing dashboard design, DAX measures, and business storytelling.

### 1. Bank Customer Analytics 2025
- Customer churn dashboard: churn % by geography, age band, gender, credit score, and product count.
- Key insight: churn is highest among customers with only 1 product and lower balances; Germany shows the highest churn rate among the three geographies.
- File: `BANK_CUSTOMER_ANALYTICS_2025.pdf`

### 2. Chronic Kidney Disease Analysis Dashboard
- Clinical analytics dashboard tracking CKD event rates against hypertension, diabetes, and smoking history.
- Includes average eGFR/creatinine baselines and risk factor breakdowns by stage.
- File: `CHRONIC_KIDNEY_DISEASE_ANALYSIS_DASHBOARD.pdf`

### 3. Country-Wise Sales Performance
- Multi-page retail analytics report: top/bottom countries and products by revenue, Pareto (80/20) analysis, monthly sales trends, and RFM (Recency-Frequency-Monetary) customer segmentation.
- File: `COUNTY_WISE_SALES_PERFORMANCE.pdf`
